About Us

Where we come from, where we wanna go.

Long, Long time ago...

We started as a small group of People that like to play Games togehter. We formed a Gaming Community, called it Just-Look and started ramping up our own Game-Servers. After we developed our own Arma3 - RP Server we began to expirience a huge growth in Members count. But Gaming was not all these people where intresseted in. More an more began to show intresset in Development, Design and even Managment.

Thats where the Network comes in. We changed our "only Gaming" mantalitly and opend up different Modules like Development. Shortly after we started the Roleplay Module for our own GTA Roleplay Project as well as Prime (still a mystirous what going on with that).

We aim to get more People that are interessted in all kinds of things and bring them togehter.
